# Research

Research ingestion and paper-processing tools.

## Model-invoked

Model- or user-reachable when the task needs paper lookup, paper fetching, or reference-document generation.

- **[arxiv-lookup](./arxiv-lookup/SKILL.md)** — Query arXiv metadata, find arXiv IDs by title or keyword, and retrieve journal DOIs from arXiv records.
- **[arxiv-doc-builder](./arxiv-doc-builder/SKILL.md)** — Fetch arXiv source/PDF materials and convert papers into Markdown reference documents.

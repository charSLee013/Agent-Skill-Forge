# Resources Format

`RESOURCES.md` is the curated source list for a course. Prefer primary sources, official documentation, trusted experts, peer-reviewed work, and high-signal communities.

## Structure

```md
# {Topic} Resources

## Knowledge

- [Source title](https://example.com)
  Covers: {the concepts, evidence, or procedures it supports}
  Use when: {the course situation that calls for it}

## Wisdom

- [Community or local resource](https://example.com)
  Offers: {the practical perspective it contributes}
  Use when: {the situation that calls for it}

## Source Gaps

- {A mission-relevant topic that needs a trusted source}
```

Annotate every source with its contribution and use case. Keep the list focused on the mission and record community preferences for future sessions.
